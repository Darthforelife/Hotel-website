Why us?

If you are the person, that wants to come out of your everyday comfort zone, you should consider staying with us. You can get to see amazing nature and wildlife, and there are alot of activities, you can do. Or you can just explore the mountains. We are a 4 star hotel, so you dont have to think we dont appreciate comfort, cause we do. Our hotel lays in the southern part of Alaska called Petersville 250 kilometers from Anchorage, where we're sure you'll have a great time.
